Youth Ministry

Raising Up Empowered Believers

Acknowledging teenagers or youth is a fairly new idea. In scripture and many other cultures, they are treated as adults in training. The removal of responsibility has not done justice for this age group. Therefore, we are not aiming to entertain old kids, but to raise up young leaders. We want equipped and empowered believers able to defend their faith and edify the body of Christ.

Equipped Defenders

Our youth program is designed to address common issues as well as scriptural truths to equip believers. We have studied through the Armor of God, the lineage of Christ, and modern hot topics. Our goal in all of it has been to better understand how God works and how we can participate in His plan.

We invite you to
serve Christ in love.
