Foundations

Foundations is our kids program on Wednesday evenings. It teaches 12 foundational attributes for the Christian walk by going through the Bible and seeing the impact of the attributes on individuals' lives.

Something New

After examining an array of kids' programs, Pastor Brian felt many of the programs out there fell short for the needs of today's kids. Our culture is saturated with talk of violence, wrath, greed, etc. Superhero movies and merchandise are everywhere. Bible stories are often told in a way that either drones on or sounds too outlandish in comparison. Many teens are leaving the church because the Bible no longer seems practical or relevant. So we decided to try something different.

What We Teach

We believe that we need to raise up a generation of equipped believers. So, we consider it best to start by teaching pillars of being upright, responsible followers of Christ. Rather than focusing on stories, we start with traits. We journey through the Bible exploring how people reacted to their circumstances and how having a particular attribute affected their situation. We then learn a verse and an answer that reinforce the topic for the month.

How it Works

In January, we study integrity and start at the beginning with Adam and Eve. Rather than getting caught up in a story that this world works to tear apart, we focus on the actions of people. Did Adam and Eve show integrity or not? How did it affect their lives? How does having integrity affect our lives? We discuss what each attribute is, what it looks like in action, and why they matter in our lives.

The Rest

In addition to teaching the attributes, we try to address modern issues. We start each week by serving dinner and end each week by teaching a practical skill. We desire most to raise up a respectable generation that follows God in such a way that others see Christ in our lives.
