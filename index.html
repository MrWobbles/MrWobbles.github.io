Our Ministries

Kid's Ministry

Foundations

Our children's ministry Foundations is designed for learning to run the race in our modern world. It focuses on attributes a Christian should have by looking at examples of believers in God found in scripture and reinforces with memory verses and concise answers to relevent questions. It rounds out by teaching life skills that are frequently lacking in today's culture.

Youth Ministry

Relevant Belief

Our youth program rotates between such classes as Relevant Belief and Apologetics. Given the cultural worldview inundating our younger generations, we are aiming to enable our youth to stand on God's Word and definitively express their beliefs.

VBS

Answers in Genesis

We are excited to present Answers in Genesis' Zoomerang VBS June 20-24. We will have it from 6:00 to 8:30 with dinner and registration beforehand at 5:30. On June 11, we will be having a block party and VBS promotion.

Fellowship Time

The Christian journey often can seem long and tough, but we are not called to go it alone. We are surrounded by a great cloud of witnesses which enable us to ease our load. Many Christians however seem to have forgotten this. When it is time to teach, we strive to endeavor in God's word with full diligence, but we need a complete walk in Christ. As such, we have opportunities to build bonds as a family and promote God-honouring friendships that can encourage us along the way.
